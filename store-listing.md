## Long description

**Dibs** is a garden planning tool for Figma built around the Square Foot Gardening (SFG) method — a proven technique that divides raised beds into one-foot squares, each planted with the right number of crops for maximum yield in minimum space.

**Create beds**
Set your bed's dimensions in feet and Dibs draws it on your canvas instantly — each square foot becomes a selectable cell. Build multiple beds on the same page to plan a full garden.

**Plant from a curated library**
Choose from 39 built-in plants across 8 categories: Fruiting Veg, Brassicas, Greens, Root Veg, Alliums, Legumes, Herbs, and Companion flowers. Each plant includes its square foot spacing, growing notes, and a color-coded swatch.

**Add your own plants**
Not in the list? Add custom plants with a name, emoji, color, and spacing. Custom plants are saved to your device and available every time you open the plugin.

**Assign and visualize**
Select one or more cells, pick a plant, and assign it. The cell fills with the plant's color and shows the emoji, name, and spacing — giving you an instant visual overview of your whole bed.

**Export a plant list**
When your beds are planned, export a CSV with every plant, cell count, total quantity needed, and growing notes — ready to take to the nursery.
